Privacy policy

We collect the minimum data needed to run price alerts and your wishlist: your email, saved products, and target prices. We never sell personal data to third parties.

Store price data shown on PriceRadar is aggregated from public listings, official APIs, and approved partner feeds.

Terms & conditions

PriceRadar is a price comparison service. We do not sell products directly — purchases happen on the retailer's own site, subject to their terms.

Prices, discounts and availability shown are indicative and refreshed periodically; always confirm the final price on the retailer's checkout page.

Affiliate disclosure

Some links on PriceRadar are affiliate links. If you make a purchase through them, we may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. This never affects how we rank or display prices — the lowest verified price is always shown first.
